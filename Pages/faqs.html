Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are your frying pans made of?

Our frying pans are made with high-quality stainless steel and feature a durable, non-stick coating for easy cleaning and long-lasting performance.

Do you offer any warranty or guarantee on your products?

Yes, we offer a 5-year warranty on all of our frying pans. If you experience any issues with your pan, please don't hesitate to contact us for a replacement or repair.

Can I purchase your products online?

Absolutely! You can purchase our frying pans and other kitchen products directly from our website. We offer fast and free shipping on all orders.

Do you offer any price or workshops?

Yes, we have a range of price programs and workshops available for both home cooks and professional chefs. You can find more information on our price page.
